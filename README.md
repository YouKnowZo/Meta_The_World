# Meta The World
### Hyper-real, sovereign, ownable reality

</div>

## Vision
- **Everything our world offers, upgraded:** Every city, biome, profession, and social system is mirrored, then enhanced with programmable physics, AI lifeforms, instant travel, perfect accessibility, and optional fantasy overlays. If it exists here, it exists there—only with better agency, safety, and creativity knobs.
- **Be who you couldn't be IRL:** Identity skins, resumable life-path simulations, and role certifications let anyone pursue careers, skills, or lifestyles they never had time, access, or resources for—without grinding through real-world gatekeeping.
- **Player-owned economy:** Land, brands, AI companions, and services are user-built and user-monetized. Smart-contract rails guarantee residual income and secondary royalties forever.
- **Hyper-real immersion:** Multi-sensory rendering (light, audio, haptics, scent) and persistent digital twins push beyond photorealism, augmented with adaptive AI weather, population, and ecology models.

## Foundational Pillars
1. **Presence Fidelity:** Real-time path-traced lighting, volumetric humans, neural radiance fields, biome-accurate audio occlusion, and full-body haptics.
2. **Living Simulation:** Agent-based ecology, supply chains, governance, and demographics constantly evolve unless players intervene.
3. **Creation at the Speed of Thought:** Natural-language + gesture tooling converts ideas into environments, quests, avatars, and props backed by AI co-creators.
4. **Ownership & Interoperability:** Every asset is an on-chain primitive (ERC-6551 accounts for avatars, ERC-721/1155 land, ERC-20 services), portable across worlds and L2s.
5. **Ethical-by-Design:** Consent layers, safety rails, age-tuned shards, and transparent governance charters keep the “better world” promise credible.

## Hyper-Real Tech Stack
- **Visual:** Neural radiance caches, RTX path tracing, foveated rendering, eye-tracking upscalers, neural materials library.
- **Physicality:** Soft-body + fluid solvers tuned to Earth values with sliders for “idealized” physics per shard.
- **Sensory:** Spatial audio with material-aware reverb, proxy scent emitters, multi-zone haptic suits, optional neural IO.
- **Simulation Cloud:** Hybrid CPU/GPU cluster + custom ASIC physics pods, streaming deterministic states via latency-aware edge nodes (<15 ms target).
- **Intelligence Layer:** Large-scene world models predict crowd flows, economy balances, and adapt story beats; agentic NPCs remember users and negotiate autonomously.

## Land & Real Estate Layer
### World Layout
- **Continental Rings:** 12 macro-biomes (urban, coastal, alpine, desert, jungle, lunar, aquatic, etc.) each with 1:1 Earth analog + “Ascended” variant.
- **Vertical Stacks:** Ground, mid-air, orbit, and abstract layers share geospatial anchors, enabling multi-level parcel ownership.
- **Dynamic Scarcity:** Base land supply mirrors real Earth parcels; expansion votes can unlock new rings, but only when usage hits 85%.

### Parcel Types

| Frontier | Experimental physics, lore shards | Unlimited (unlocked via quests) | For avant-garde mechanics |

### Virtual Real Estate Agent Path
1. **Licensing Sim:** 4-hour mixed-reality course covering compliance, cultural stewardship, and deal mechanics; NFT license minted on pass.
2. **Portfolio Builder:** Curated dashboard with live heatmaps (foot traffic, retention, spend), AI comps, and cinematic flythrough capture tools.
3. **Client Matchmaking:** Intent graph pairs agents with builders, brands, or settlers; multilingual AI concierges prep briefs.
4. **Deal Rooms:** Smart-contract-backed negotiation spaces with shared holographic models, legal templates, and escrow bots.
5. **Commission Engine:** Default 2.0% agent fee on primary sales, 0.25% lifetime override on secondary trades routed through their listings. Referral trees reward community-building.
6. **Reputation & Progression:** XP from satisfaction scores, upkeep missions, and dispute resolutions unlocks exclusive districts and co-development slots.

## Economy & Transactions
- **Currency Stack:** Stable settlement token (`META:USD`) + floating creative token (`META:FLOW`) + fiat on/off ramps.
- **Transaction Flow:** Presence contract → escrow vault → multi-party signature (buyer, seller, agent, validator) → instant disbursement + royalty splits.
- **Compliance Layer:** Built-in AML/KYC modules, geo-fencing, age gating, and cultural heritage protections for iconic locations.
- **Microservices for Agents:** CRM APIs, lead scoring, analytics SDK, commission tracking, and automated tax exports.

## Creation & Identity
- **Avatar Fabricator:** Blend scans, narrative archetypes, or purely fictional species; identity layers toggle between professional, social, and anonymous masks.
- **Experience Composer:** Speak+sketch to generate environments; AI verifies structural soundness + vibe alignment.
- **Time Control:** Personal shards support pause/rewind; shared public spaces run on synchronized “Prime Time” but can fork into story instances.

## Governance & Safety
- **Tri-Camera Governance:** Token holders, verified creators, and civic stewards each hold 1/3 vote weight on major proposals.
- **Trust Fabric:** Real-time behavior scoring, consent tokens, restorative justice quests instead of perma-bans.
- **Moderation AI:** Context-aware models escalate sensitive events to human guardians; privacy-preserving audit trails.

4. **Phase 3 – Ascension Era (36+ months):** Unlock frontier physics shards, neural streaming, and fully programmable societal systems.
