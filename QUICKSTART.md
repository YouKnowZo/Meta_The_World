## 📝 Your First Real Estate Sale

### Step-by-Step:

1. **Start the interactive demo:**
   ```bash
   python3 demo.py
   ```

2. **Create your character:**
   - Enter your name
   - Choose "Real Estate Agent" as profession
   - You start with $10,000

3. **Browse properties:**
   - Select option 1: "Browse Properties"
   - View available listings
   - Note the commission you'll earn

4. **Make your first sale:**
   - Choose option 2: "My Real Estate Business"
   - Or view property details
   - Select "Find a buyer"
   - **Earn your first commission!** 💰

5. **Watch your wealth grow:**
   - Every sale earns you 5% commission
   - Make 10 sales to level up
   - Higher levels = Higher commission rates (up to 8%)

## 📊 Understanding Your Stats

### Real Estate Agent Dashboard:
- **License Level** - Your experience (1-10+)
- **Commission Rate** - Percentage you earn (5%-8%)
- **Total Sales** - Number of properties sold
- **Sales Volume** - Total value of all sales
- **Total Commissions** - Your earnings
- **Reputation** - How well-known you are

### Level Up System:
- Level 1: 0 sales needed, 5.0% commission
- Level 2: 10 sales needed, 5.5% commission
- Level 3: 20 sales needed, 6.0% commission
- Level 4: 30 sales needed, 6.5% commission
- Level 5: 40 sales needed, 7.0% commission
- Level 10: 90 sales needed, 8.0% commission

## 🆘 Troubleshooting

### "Insufficient funds"
- Buyers need more money
- Try cheaper properties first
- Or wait for wealthier buyers

### "Property not found"
- Property might be sold
- Search for more listings
- Time will generate new interest
