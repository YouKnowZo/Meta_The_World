# 🚀 MetaWorld Quick Start Guide

Get started in MetaWorld in under 5 minutes!

## Step 1: Install (1 minute)

```bash
npm install
```

## Step 2: Launch (30 seconds)

```bash
npm start
```

Open your browser to: **http://localhost:3000**

## Step 3: Your First Steps in MetaWorld (3 minutes)

### 1. Choose Your Profession ⭐
**Recommended for beginners: Real Estate Agent**

- Look at the left panel
- Find the "Choose Your Path" dropdown
- Select "Real Estate Agent (5% commission)"
- 🎉 You now earn 5% on every property deal!

### 2. Explore the World 🌍

- Move your mouse over buildings - they light up!
- Click on any building to view property details
- The camera will smoothly fly to the property
- Notice the beautiful 3D city around you

### 3. Make Your First Purchase 💰

**Best starter properties (under $200,000):**
- Cozy Apartments: ~$150,000
- Mountain Cabins: ~$200,000

**How to buy:**
1. Click a building or find it in the "Real Estate Market" panel (right side)
2. Click "Show Property" or "Buy Property"
3. Review the transaction details
4. **Notice your 5% commission** (you earn money while buying!)
5. Click "Confirm"

### 4. Build Your Empire 📈

After your first purchase:
- Your commission is added to your earnings
- You level up based on transaction size
- Property appears in "Your Properties" (left panel, bottom)
- Your net worth increases
- You can buy more properties!

## Pro Tips 💡

1. **Commission Magic**: As a Real Estate Agent, a $200,000 property only costs you $190,000 (you get $10,000 commission back!)

2. **Level Up Bonuses**: Every level gives you cash bonuses. Buy bigger properties for faster leveling!

3. **Portfolio Strategy**: 
   - Start with 3-5 smaller properties
   - Then move to luxury properties
   - Aim for variety in property types

4. **Chat with Others**: Use the chat in the bottom-left to connect with other players

5. **Watch the Market**: Property availability changes as others buy

## Controls 🎮

- **Mouse Hover**: Highlight buildings
- **Click Building**: View and purchase property
- **Camera**: Automatically moves to selected properties
- **UI Panels**: All clickable - explore everything!

## Understanding Your Stats 📊

**Top Bar:**
- 💰 Balance: Your available cash
- 🏆 Level: Experience level (increases with transactions)
- 💼 Profession: Your current job

**Left Panel (Your Profile):**
- Properties Owned: Count of properties you own
- Total Investments: Value of all properties
- Total Earnings: All money earned
- Commission Earned: Money from being a realtor

**Right Panel (Market):**
- Available properties to purchase
- Real-time property listings

A: Yes! Just select a different one from the dropdown.

**Q: What if I run out of money?**
A: Level up bonuses give you money, or start with smaller properties to earn commission.

**Q: How do I earn the most money?**
A: As a Real Estate Agent, buy properties to get commission. Each purchase actually earns you money!

**Q: Can I sell properties?**

**Q: What's the best property?**

## Troubleshooting 🔧

**Nothing loads?**
- Make sure Node.js is installed
- Check that port 3000 is available
- Try: `npm install` then `npm start`

**3D doesn't show?**
- Use Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge
- Enable WebGL in browser settings
- Update graphics drivers

**Laggy performance?**
- Close other browser tabs
- Reduce window size
- Use a more powerful device
