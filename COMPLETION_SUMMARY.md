## 💡 Key Innovation: Commission System

The **Real Estate Agent** profession is the star feature:

**Example Transaction:**
- Property Price: $200,000
- Your Commission (5%): **+$10,000**
- Net Cost to You: **$190,000**
- Your Balance After: **$810,000** (started with $1M)

**You actually EARN money while buying properties!**

This creates a compelling gameplay loop where:
1. Buy property → Earn commission
2. Use commission → Buy more properties
3. Earn more → Level up faster
4. Repeat → Build empire
